    14,18 millions

    Tokyo Disney Resort is a magical paradise that combines Disney fantasy with Japanese finesse. The resort has two unique theme parks: Tokyo Disneyland and Tokyo DisneySea. Both offer thrilling rides, breathtaking shows and an attention to detail that will leave you in awe. The fusion of cultures makes this destination an unforgettable experience for any visitor.
